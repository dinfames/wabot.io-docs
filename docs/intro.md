# Wabot.io Documentation

**The complete guide to Wabot.io — the all-in-one WhatsApp automation platform.**

_Panduan lengkap untuk Wabot.io — platform automasi WhatsApp serba lengkap._

---

## Welcome / Selamat Datang

**Wabot.io** is a WhatsApp automation platform that helps businesses turn conversations into loyal customers — without writing a single line of code. From simple order notifications to AI chatbots that close sales 24/7, Wabot gives you one workspace for sales, support, and automation.

**Wabot.io** ialah platform automasi WhatsApp yang membantu perniagaan menukar perbualan kepada pelanggan setia — tanpa menulis sebarang kod. Daripada notifikasi pesanan sehingga chatbot AI yang menutup jualan 24/7, Wabot memberi anda satu ruang kerja untuk jualan, sokongan, dan automasi.

---

## What You Can Do with Wabot

- **[Notifications](/docs/features/broadcast)** — Send order confirmations, shipping updates, and payment reminders automatically.
- **[Broadcast](/docs/features/broadcast)** — Run marketing campaigns to thousands of contacts with scheduling and targeting.
- **[AI Chatbots](/docs/features/chatbots)** — Deploy AI that answers FAQs, qualifies leads, and closes sales 24/7.
- **[Automation](/docs/features/automation)** — Build visual drag-and-drop flows triggered by webhooks or WhatsApp labels.
- **[Live Chat](/docs/features/live-chat)** — Shared team inbox for WhatsApp with AI-to-human handoff.
- **[Autoresponder](/docs/features/autoresponder)** — Keyword-based auto-replies for greetings, FAQs, and quick support.

## Quick Start

1. **[Sign up](/docs/getting-started/signup)** — Create a free account at [app.wabot.io](https://app.wabot.io).
2. **[Connect WhatsApp](/docs/getting-started/connect-account)** — Scan a QR code to link your number.
3. **[Send your first message](/docs/getting-started/first-message)** — Test that everything works.
4. **[Build a chatbot or broadcast](/docs/features/)** — Start automating.
