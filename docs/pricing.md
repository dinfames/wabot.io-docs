# Pricing & Plans

Wabot offers five plans in Malaysian Ringgit (RM). All plans include a free trial, no credit card required.

## Plan Comparison

| Feature | Lite | Basic | Plus | Premier | Platinum |
|---------|:----:|:-----:|:----:|:-------:|:--------:|
| **Price (RM/month)** | 30 | 60 | 120 | 290 | 890 |
| **WhatsApp Numbers** | 1 | 1 | 1 | 3 | 10 |
| **Messages/month** | 30,000 | 100,000 | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| **Max file upload** | — | 2 MB | 10 MB | 15 MB | 25 MB |
| **Storage** | — | 100 MB | 300 MB | 500 MB | 2 GB |
| **AI Credits/month** | — | — | 3,000 | 10,000 | 50,000 |
| **Notifications** | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Broadcast** | No | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Keyword Auto-Reply** | No | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **AI Chatbot** | No | No | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Automation** | No | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Live Chat** | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **VVIP Support** | No | No | No | No | Yes |

**Plus** is the most popular plan.

## Free Trial

Every new account starts with a **free trial**:

- No credit card required
- Basic features unlocked for testing
- Sample quota for test messages
- Upgrade anytime

## Upgrading / Downgrading

From the Dashboard → **Current Package** card → click **Manage Subscription**. Or go to:

```
https://fatomate.com/sp/pricing-wabot
```

Changes apply at the next billing cycle.

## Top-Up AI Credits

If you exceed your monthly AI credit allowance:

- **Purchase additional credits** from the Dashboard → AI Credits card → **Purchase**
- Or enable **Advance credits** (overdraft billing)
