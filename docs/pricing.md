## Which Plan Should I Pick?

=== "I just need order notifications"

    **→ Lite (RM30/mo)**

    - 1 WhatsApp number
    - 30,000 messages/month
    - Notifications only (no broadcast, no AI, no automation)

=== "I want to send monthly promo blasts"

    **→ Basic (RM60/mo)**

    - 1 number
    - 100,000 messages/month
    - Broadcast + keyword auto-replies + notifications
    - Good for small online stores

=== "I want AI to close sales for me"

    **→ Plus (RM120/mo)** ⭐ Most Popular

    - 1 number
    - Unlimited messages
    - AI Chatbot (3,000 credits/month)
    - Full automation builder
    - Best for solo entrepreneurs and small teams

=== "I run 2-3 brands or need more AI"

    **→ Premier (RM290/mo)**

    - 3 WhatsApp numbers (~RM96 per number)
    - Unlimited messages
    - 10,000 AI credits/month
    - Ideal for 1,000–3,000 leads/month

=== "I'm an enterprise with high volume"

    **→ Platinum (RM890/mo)**

    - 10 WhatsApp numbers (~RM89 per number)
    - Unlimited messages
    - 50,000 AI credits/month
    - VVIP private group support
    - For 3,000–10,000 leads/month

## Free Trial

Every new account starts with a **free trial**:

- No credit card required
- Basic features unlocked for testing
- Sample quota for test messages
- Upgrade anytime

## Upgrading / Downgrading

From the Dashboard → **Current Package** card → click **Manage Subscription**. Or go to:

```
https://fatomate.com/sp/pricing-wabot
```

Changes apply at the next billing cycle.

## Top-Up AI Credits

If you exceed your monthly AI credit allowance:

- **Purchase additional credits** from the Dashboard → AI Credits card → **Purchase**
- Or enable **Advance credits** (overdraft billing)
